## Project

A web-based re-creation of Life-4-Cuts (인생네컷), a popular South Korean photo booth experience. The app should allow users to take a series of 4 (or more, or less) photos with filters and print/download them in the classic photo strip format.

## Status

This project is in early setup — no build system, dependencies, or source files have been created yet.
